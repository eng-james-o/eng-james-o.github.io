Airfm

A software for drone builders to create, manipulate, analyse and compare airfoils, to simplify the workflow of designing and building drones.

Key Features:

  • Airfoil creation and editing tools
  • Comparative analysis of airfoil performance
  • Visualization and export options
  • Designed for drone and RC aircraft builders
